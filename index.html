PIZZA

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a flattened, leavened bread dough base topped with savory ingredients, most commonly tomato sauce and cheese, and various other toppings like meats and vegetables, then baked at a high temperature. It originated in Naples, Italy, and has since become a globally recognized and diverse food, served hot and often without utensils

BURGER

A burger, is a sandwich consisting of a cooked patty served between two halves of a bun, often with toppings like lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, and condiments such as ketchup and mustard

NOODLES

Noodles are long, thin strips or strings made from unleavened dough, most commonly formed from wheat or rice flour, but also other starches and grains. They serve as a staple food in many cultures and are prepared in various ways, such as boiling in water or soup, pan-frying, or baking in casseroles, and are often served with sauces or other ingredients.

PANIPURI

Pani Puri, also known as Golgappa or Puchka, is a popular Indian street food consisting of hollow, crispy-fried dough balls (puris) filled with a mixture of spiced potato, chickpeas, and other ingredients, then topped with a flavorful, tangy, and spicy water (pani). The resulting dish offers a burst of complex flavors and textures, typically eaten in a single bite to experience the full combination of sweet, sour, and spicy elements

MOMOS

Momos are a type of dumpling native to Himalayan communities like Tibet and Nepal, popular across South Asia, especially in India and Bhutan. They consist of a thin dough wrapper filled with various ingredients, vegetables seasoned with spices and herbs. Momos are typically prepared by steaming but can also be fried, pan-fried, or even served in a soup. They are usually accompanied by a spicy dipping sauce or chutney.
